About Me

Currently working as Product Owner with heavy focus on the technical side, specifically data engineering and science.
The health sector still lacks innovation, with healthcare services constantly failing throughout the world and no value being extracted from the data.
My main goal is to use this skills to extract value from real world data (RWD) collected from patients and clinicians, by developing powerful end-to-end data pipelines, from source to harmonization to analysis.
By doing so, I hope to make a positive impact in the world of health bringing patients, healthcare professionals, and tech closer together.
